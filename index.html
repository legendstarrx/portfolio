Creative testing process

I do not throw random creatives at an account. I research what is working, build hooks around the offer, test one clear angle at a time, and read the numbers: hook rate, outbound CTR, CPC, LPV rate, conversion rate, CPL, and CPA. Winners get more variations. Losers get cut.
